About Us


SAMYAMA SAHYADRI TECH LLP incorporated on 30 April, 2019 at Sahyadri College of Engineering and Management initiates “Aasha”, an endeavor for Early Detection of Breast Cancer by Thermal Imaging. Our objective is to contribute to the Sustainable Development Goal 3, regarding "Good Health and Well-being", and serve every woman, reach every society and fight against breast cancer by doing whatever it takesWith Aasha we intend to serve every women, reach every community and deliver a painless, noninvasive and affordable screening technique.
